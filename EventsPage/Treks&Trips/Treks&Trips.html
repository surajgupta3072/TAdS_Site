Bhutan Road Trip, First ever International Trip organized by TAdS

Bhutan, a Buddhist kingdom on the Himalayas’ eastern edge, known for its monasteries, f​​ortresses, and dramatic landscapes that range from subtropical plains to steep mountains and valleys. In the High Himalayas, peaks such as 7,326m Jomolhari are popular trekking destinations. Paro Taktsang monastery also known as Tiger’s Nest clings to cliffs above the forested Paro Valley. Enjoy the 2,500 Km cross-country road trip with scenic beauty all around.
